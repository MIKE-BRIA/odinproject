choma

Nyama choma with roasted potato

this is a typical meal in most african countries but mainly popularised in kenya and mainly found in kitengela

these are the list of ingredients

  • 1/2kg meat
  • potato
  • 1/4 litre oil
  • onions

steps

  • clean the meat and boil for 10 minutes at a temperature of about 60 degrees
  • make an open fire and place the meat on the fire with the help of meat holders
  • salt the meat as you pell the outter cover of the potato
  • as the meat gets roasted prepare another fire place to make the potato
  • roast the potato for 15 minutes
  • cut the meat to peaces then cut onions and tomato on a different plate to make kachumbari
